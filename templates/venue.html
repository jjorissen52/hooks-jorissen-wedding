Awesome Hall lies within the remains of an ancient moated site, containing several medieval and post medieval timber framed and thatched barns and other traditional farm structures originally used by a wealthy farmstead.

Awesome Hall is steeped in history. It is still possible to see where the original moat runs which pre dates most of the existing farmstead and can be seen as a large dry ditch and shallow depression and a further part is perpetuated as one of the ponds.
